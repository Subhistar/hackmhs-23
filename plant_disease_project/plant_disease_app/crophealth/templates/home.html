Indentifation tool for spotting diseases in plants and crops

About the software

The intent of this software was to improve the sustainability of crops by allowing farmers to be able to indetify disease or malnourishments in their plants and crops. By using image recognition software, we hope to decrease the number of crops lost to harmful diseases and infestations, and hope to help large farmers and small gardeners alike to protect their plants.
